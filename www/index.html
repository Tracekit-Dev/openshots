Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Professional tools without the price tag or the bloat.

📷

Capture Anything

Full screen, region, or window capture. Multi-monitor support with stitched displays. Configurable global hotkeys and system tray - always one shortcut away.

🎨

Stunning Backgrounds

Linear and radial gradients, solid colors, or your own images. macOS wallpapers built-in. Adjustable blur for depth. Fan layout for multi-image compositions.

✏️

Professional Annotations

Arrows with curvature control, rectangles, ellipses, text, and full emoji picker. 10-color curated palette. Inter font with halo shadows. Double-click to edit text inline.

🔐

Privacy & Precision

Blur or cover sensitive regions with adjustable opacity and custom colors. Flip, rotate, and resize with precision. Everything runs locally - nothing leaves your machine.

📦

Flexible Export

PNG, JPEG, or WebP at 1x, 2x, or 3x scale. One-click clipboard copy. Save reusable presets to apply your style instantly across screenshots.

⚡

Fast & Lightweight

Built with Tauri and Rust. Under 20MB installer - 5x smaller than Electron alternatives. Native performance on Mac, Windows, and Linux.

🤖

CLI for Agents & Scripts

A full command-line interface for batch processing with preset support. Run it from scripts, or let AI agents like Claude Code generate polished visuals automatically. Coming soon.

🔓

Open Source

Fully open source. Audit the code, contribute features, or fork it. No vendor lock-in, no telemetry, no hidden costs. Free forever for personal use.
