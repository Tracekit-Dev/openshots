Everything you need

📷

Capture Anything

Full screen, region selection, or specific window. Global hotkeys work from anywhere. System tray for quick access.

🎨

Beautiful Backgrounds

Vibrant gradients, solid colors, or upload your own image. Use macOS wallpapers built-in. Add blur and grain effects.

✏️

Annotate

Arrows, rectangles, ellipses, text, and emoji. Drag, resize, and rotate. Full undo/redo with keyboard shortcuts.

🔐

Privacy First

Blur or pixelate sensitive areas. Everything runs locally — your screenshots never leave your machine.

📦

Export Anywhere

PNG, JPEG, or WebP at 1x, 2x, or 3x. Copy to clipboard in one click. Save and reuse presets.

⚡

Fast & Lightweight

Built with Tauri and Rust. Under 20MB installer. Native performance. No Electron bloat.
